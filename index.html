EPL Coach Selector

How To Play

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.
There are four coaches below. By clicking on a coach you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the randomly generated number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.
The value of each coach is hidden from you until you click on it.
Each time the game is started, the values of each coach will change.
